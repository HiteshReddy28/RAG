H1: G-1650,Authorization for ACH Transactions

Use Form G-1650, Authorization for ACH Transactions, to pay the filing fees for an application, petition, or request that you are filing with USCIS by Automated Clearing House (ACH) transaction. Form G-1650 allows you to pay your fees electronically using bank account information instead of mailing a paper check or money order. We will use the information on your Form G-1650 to debit your bank account directly.

The account must be with a U.S. bank. We cannot accept an ACH transaction issued by a foreign bank. There is no additional cost to pay your filing fee by ACH transaction.

Complete Form G-1650 and place it on top of your application, petition, or request. Mail your application, petition, or request to the filing location listed in the “Where to File” section of the webpage for the form you are submitting.

File Form G-1650 when you want to pay your fee electronically or when we require an electronic payment method.

There is no fee for Form G-1650. You can find USCIS filing fees by visiting ourFee Schedulepage.

By completing this transaction, you agree to pay for a government service and that the fees and all related financial transactions are final and not refundable, regardless of any action USCIS takes on an application, petition, or request. You must submit all fees in the exact amounts.
