H1: Direct Filing Addresses for Form I-360, Immigrant Petition for Amerasian, Widow(er) or Special Immigrant

Alert:Before sending your package to USCIS, make sure that all the forms you have completed are the current acceptable edition. The current acceptable edition can be found under the Edition Date section listed on each form’s webpage.

You can also find the edition date at the bottom of the page on the form and instructions. For each form that you are submitting, make sure that all pages are from the same form edition. If any of the form’s pages are from a different form edition, we may reject your form. WatchUSCIS Forms: Download the most recent version!to see how to ensure your form is acceptable.

For additional guidance on filing paper-based forms at the Lockbox and how to avoid or correct common errors before you file, please visit theFive Steps to File at the USCIS Lockboxwebpage.

ALERT:On Oct. 14, 2025, we changed the filing location for certain petitioners filingForm I-360, Petition for Amerasian, Widow(er), or Special Immigrant. If you are filing Form I-360 under the VAWA program together with aForm I-485, Application to Register Permanent Residence or Adjust Status, postmarked on or after Oct. 14, 2025, you must file this form at our USCIS Lockbox locations, instead of Service Center Operations - Nebraska. For more information, see the filing location information below.

We will provide a 30-day grace period for forms mailed to Service Center Operations - Nebraska. Service Center Operations - Nebraska will reject any Form I-360 under the VAWA program filed together with a Form I-485, postmarked on or after Nov. 14, 2025.

If you live outside the U.S., you cannot file Form I-485, Application to Register Permanent Residence or Adjust Status, either concurrently or after we approve your Form I-360. Instead, you may apply for an immigrant visa at a U.S. Consulate if we approve your Form I-360.

Petitions that are not mailed to the appropriate filing location may experience processing delays.
