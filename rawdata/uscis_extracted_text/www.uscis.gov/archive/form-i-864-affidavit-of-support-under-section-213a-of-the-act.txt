H1: Form I-864, Affidavit of Support Under Section 213A of the Act

The information on this page is out of date. However, some of the content may still be useful, so we have archived the page.

U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) Public Engagement Division and the National Benefits Center invited you to participate in a stakeholder teleconference on June 26, 2013, at 12:30 p.m. (Eastern) to discuss Form I-864,Affidavit of Support.

During the engagement, USCIS subject matter experts presented an overview of Form I-864, offer filing tips, and addressed the primary reasons for requests for evidence. Stakeholders had an opportunity to ask questions and to provide feedback on Form I-864.
