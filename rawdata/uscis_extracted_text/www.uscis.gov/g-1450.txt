H1: G-1450,Authorization for Credit Card Transactions

You may pay your filing fee and biometric services fee, if applicable,with a credit cardissued by a U.S. bank, if you are filing:

- An application, petition, or request with a USCIS lockbox (except Form I-956, Application for Regional Center Designation, and Form I-956F, Application for Approval of an Investment in a Commercial Enterprise); or

- An application, petition, or request with the USCIS service centers.

There is no additional cost to file by credit card. We cannot accept a credit card issued by a foreign bank.
